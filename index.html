Game-Rules:

  • The Game Has Two Players, Playing in Rounds.
  • In each turn , a Player rolls his Dice as many times as he wishes. Each result gets added to his ROUND Score.
  • But if a Player rolls 1, All his ROUND Score will be lost, And its next Player's turn.
  • The Player can choose to 'HOLD', That means that his ROUND Score will be Added to his GLOBAL Score, Then its Next Player's turn.
  • The First Player to Reach 100 Points in GLOBAL Score Wins the Match.
